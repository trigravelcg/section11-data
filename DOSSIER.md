## 5. Nutrition Strategy

**On-Bike Fueling**

- **Drink Mix:** EFS-Pro — **2 scoops per 25oz bottle = 240 calories**
- **Gels:** First Endurance Liquid Shot (120 calories each)
  - Non-caffeinated for first half of ride
  - Caffeinated versions for later in longer events
- **Solid Food:** Hammer Bars (only when needed to kill hunger — usually just one per long ride)
- **General Rule:** Aim for 60–90g carbs/hour on rides >90 minutes. Higher end (80–100g/hr) on very long / high TSS days.

**Short Endurance Days (<2 hours):**  
Usually one bottle of EFS-Pro + 1–2 gels.

**Long Endurance / Race Days (>3 hours):**  
- Start with EFS-Pro in bottles
- Gels every 30–45 minutes
- One Hammer Bar if hunger appears
- Adjust based on heat, intensity, and GI tolerance
